Milady Maker is a collection of 10,000 generative pfpNFT's in a neochibi aesthetic with randomized cosmetics inspired by 00's Tokyo street fashion. Under the vision of digital artist Milady Sonora, Milady's are designed to be fashion-minded and genuinely good social media avatars, and an invitation into their personal aesthetic world. Owning a Milady provides access to the Milady Village members-only community, and special rights to planned expansions: Milady Banner, a tie-in generative NFT cover photo series, Milady Community Radio, a community governance digital radio dApp, and Milady Maker 2, an avatar cosmetics market.

Details:

  • Fair launch: Collection dropped simultaneously at a 0.08 E mint fee. No new Milady's will be minted.
  • Avatar-first design: Illustrated from the beginning to ensure strong social media profile aesthetics with an emphasis on fashion. Ties into the planned generative NFT Header.
  • Milady Village:Member's only Discord community, with a planned community radio and mixtape series, and designated as a launchpad for Milady Maker 2.
  • Graded for Drip: Attempting to move beyond randomized scarcity, cosmetic rarity was scaled by their drip factor and visual prominence, and Milady's are providing ranking badges based on a weighted grading of their drip, their adherence to a single style theme, as well as the objective rarity of their specific cosmetics.
  • Chain roll function: Randomized cosmetics are organized into sartorial themes, with a chain algorithm at the end of each cosemtic generation adding a 20% chance of the consecutive generation sticking to the same theme, designed to organically produce aesthetic coherency

Roadmap:

  1. Milady Banners: Generative NFT header photo series, mintably only to Milady NFT owners; designed to matching aesthetically with Milady Maker as an extension of its avatar-first design.
  2. Milady Community Radio: Decentralized digital radio station designed to stream album-by-album, with community governance with adding/removing albums from the mix.
  3. Milady Maker 2: High resolution pixel based render of the Milady Maker NFT's built with a secondary accessory market that allows switching and trading individual cosmetics. Gen 1 Milady's will be able to import all accessories.
  4. $MILADY: Reward token added as an extension to Milady Maker 2 farmed through a daily care minigame and used to buy and sell cosmetic accessories.

Style Themes

Eight style theme inspired by Y2K Tokyo street fashion are defined, with every cosmetic falling under one theme:

Gyaruou, Gyaru, Prep, Gothic Lolita, Pretty Lolita, Hypebeast, Grailedboy, Prep and Costume.

Every Milady can be ranked on its coherence to a theme, with a special badge being applied when total weighted accessories are single theme over 51%, 75%, 90% and 100%.

Generation Design Philosophy

Following the design of all the Milady cosmetics, a Drip Assessment was performed with the team subjectively grading each cosmetic for its level of drip - stylishnessness and luxury. This was used to decide the distribution rate for each cosmetic, with the higher drip pieces being more rare to increase their market value.

After a Milady's fashion is generated, the drip level assigned to each cosmetic is added up, weighted by the visual prominence of its slot (e.g. hat > necklace). Special borders are rewarded to Milady's if their overall drip is ranked a higher grade - drip, superdrip, legendary drip. This is designed to favor visually prominent aesthetic slots over only objective rarity of the collected cosmetics.

Biotrait Generation

Milady's base "biotraits" are randomly generated to create unique race, face & hair combinations. All biotraits maintain a standard distribution, except for a small selection of rarities and ultrararities, to allow speculative value to be focused on the cosmetics.

Metrics

Beyond simple objective rarity, every Milady can be valuated from a few set of novel angles introduced by the generative algorithm:

Drip Grade
Every accessory falls under 3 basic grades defined by their distribution rarity:

Grade Distribution
Basic Common
Drip Rare
Superdrip Ultrarare

This serves as a rough rule of thumb to catalogue Milady's into three buckets of overall accessory rarity.

Core Consistency
A Milady can also be rated by how much their total accessorization falls under a specific Core

Costume Completion
Every costume has a certain amount of items that complete it. A Milady can be scored by how close they come to completing any costume, as well as by the individual rarity of each costume.

Notes On the Design Process

Milady Sonora's Neochibi Style
Milady Sonora is a digital painter who has shown works in galleries both physically and digitally through Remilia Collective. They've developed their distinctive take on the Chibi Anime style in a portraiture series over the years, one which invokes a calming, ethereal, white-hearted angelicism in a blend of Western and Japanese aesthetics.

Sartorialism
The team was interested in approaching the generative art space from an angle of sartorialism, by using the Milady as a template for fashion accessorization. The thematic "Cores" were chosen around a loose narrative of throwback Tokyo street fashion, as seemed suited to Milady's fusion "neochibi" aesthetic. The Chain Algorithm was designed to lend the generated works towards more aesthetic coherency, something that seemed missing in earlier generative NFT projects which followed more traditional RNG.

Intuitive Value: Rarity <-> Drip
A major motivating factor for this design was also the desire for relative value to be intuitive; without knowing the 'meta' of the Milady market, one can ascertain higher or lower value Milady's at a glance by appraising their drip. This is achieved understanding the direct relationship between rarity and secondary market value; distribution rates were decided by first assessing each accessory by its "drip", making the highest drip accessories most rare and thus most valuable.

Profile-first
An interesting, unplanned development in generative NFT projects is buyers using the works as profile pictures on online communities. In retrospect, this makes sense, as the field operates almost entirely by word-of-mouth from digital networks. Unfortunately this is not something that most of the earlier generative NFT projects have designed with in mind. Mainly, they are too far zoomed out and have minimal background generation. While a user can manually crop their NFT and create a transparency to add a background, most do not; maybe out of lack of knowhow, or because its a violation of their NFT art - like drawing over a painting.

Milady's were designed to be great profile pictures from the start, with a higher vertical ratio so that a square crop will be a perfect zoom to convey emotional expressiveness, while still retaining the space to show off accessories. The design of the accessories also intentionally lent high-value rarities to larger or more distinctly colored pieces, with the intention that a valuable Milady can be read as such even as a thumbnail.

This is also why an optional NFT header add-in is planned as the gen 2 production, to cement our recognition of the unique relationship between generative NFT's and online spaces - perhaps as an early, organic structure for Ethereum based identities!

Specs
We chose to follow the standard and proven formula of generative NFT art so we could place focus on our design innovations described above: 10,000 units, ERC-720 NFT, fair single drop with price to mint, and reserves set aside for marketing & community management.
