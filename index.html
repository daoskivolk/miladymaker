Welcome to Milady Maker! — ゆっくりしていってね!

Milady Maker is a collection of 10,000 fashion-minded pfpNFT's in a neochibi aesthetic inspired by Tokyo street fashion.


Details:

  • Avatar-first design: Illustrated from the beginning to ensure strong social media profile aesthetics with an emphasis on fashion. Ties into the planned generative NFT Header.
  • Milady Village: Member's only Discord community, with a planned community radio and mixtape series, and designated as a launchpad for Milady Maker 2.
  • Chain roll function: Randomized cosmetics are organized into sartorial themes, with a chain algorithm tied to each cosmetic generation for a chance of the consecutive generation sticking to the same theme, designed to organically produce aesthetic coherency
  • Graded for Drip: Milady's have ranked badges on a variety of weighted metrics, adding additional layers to a speculative market beyond objective trait scarcity.

The Milady Maker collection will be fairly launched with a simultaneous drop at a 0.05 E mint fee. No new Gen 1 Milady's will ever be minted.

Roadmap

50% — Milady Community Radio:

Decentralized digital radio station dApp designed to stream album-by-album, with community governance with adding/removing albums from the mix.

Mixtapes are also shared to an exclusive Milady-owner only library.

75% — Milady Banners:

Generative NFT header photo series, available to mint only to Milady NFT owners. The banners are being designed with a novel generative algorithm styled to match aesthetically with Milady Maker as an extension of its avatar-first design. They will be available in multiple formats and resolution.

100% — Milady Maker 2:

High resolution pixel based render of the Milady Maker NFT's built with a secondary accessory market that allows switching and trading individual cosmetics. Gen 1 Milady's will be able to import their accessories or have it cashed into $MILADY, a reward token introduced to buy and sell cosmetic accessories, farmable in a daily care minigame.

Milady Maker 2 will handdraw accessories into a stylized pixel aesthetic.

Style Themes

Eight style theme inspired by Y2K Tokyo street fashion are defined, with every cosmetic falling under one theme:

Gyaruou, Gyaru, Prep, Gothic Lolita, Pretty Lolita, Hypebeast, Grailedboy, Prep and Costume.

Every Milady can be ranked on its coherence to a theme, with a special badge being applied when total weighted accessories are single theme over 51%, 75%, 90% and 100%.

Milady's are designed to produce eclectic, but spiritually coherent outfits.
